planet earth

Earth, the third planet from the Sun, is our unique cosmic home and the only known astronomical body to harbor life. It is an "oblate spheroid", roughly 4.5 billion years old, with a surface covered by approximately 71% liquid water and 30% land. Protected by a breathable, oxygen-rich atmosphere and a magnetic field, it sustains a vibrant, interconnected web of life.

  1. Planet Earth is the third planet from the Sun and the only world in our solar system known to harbor life
  2. Roughly \(71\%\) of its surface is covered by liquid water, which sustains its dynamic global ecosystems
  3. Scientists estimate that this vibrant, rocky planet formed approximately \(4.5\) billion years ago.
